So how do we do it?

Our comprehensive selection of premium athletic flooring solutions is built to deliver a perfect balance between performance and aesthetics to the customers. Our technical knowledge as industry leaders combined with our wide array of product options results in a turn-key service that covers every single detail minutely right from the initial concept to the stage of project completion.

We listen, evaluate and understand customer requirements and that’s how we go about each project that we undertake; caring for you at every stage of our business model.




Design

When you chose Krieda, your needs become our targets and your project, our responsibility. Our professional installation team and knowledgeable sales consultants will guide you through the selection of materials and surface that best suits your requirements and budgets in a quest to create a safe and enjoyable environment. With us, you can be assured of the services offered as our products are specifically designed to accommodate a wide range of environments and sporting events.

Build

We make use of the best products available within each type of system that not only is a pre-requisite for a fierce gameplay but also a hallmark of quality that makes us a credible name in the industry. From the very first contact, our expert team will work hand-in-hand with you to determine and build an ideal flooring solution for your needs.

Handover

Our products are made to deliver and last for decades. But as is the case with every product, flooring surfaces do require care and proper maintenance. Once customer requirements are met as per our satisfaction standards, our team will consult with the athletic directors and arena managers for surface care and guide them through the maintenance process which will ultimately save your facility money and extend the life of your invaluable asset.

Operations

From the basic tasks all the way to lighting and poles, Krieda has got your back. We acknowledge that blossoming budding superstars requires efforts and hard work, that’s why we offer our clients with an option to operate the arenas on behalf of them so that they can focus on what matters them the most- sports.
